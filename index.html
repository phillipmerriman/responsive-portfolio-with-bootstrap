About Me

I am a software developer currently living near Minneapolis, Minnesota. After touring the world in a heavy metal band for a few years, I changed my sights from performance to composition for video games and film. While in that industry, I quickly saw how much learning to code would help myself and my clients. As a student at the University of MN coding boot camp, I'm stoked to start a new journey towards my new career!

As a video game composer and fitness nerd, I am interested in:

  • Web sites and apps that fuel the gaming, music, and fitness industries.
  • Tough code that won't break.
  • Fancy guitars.
  • Home gym equipment.
  • Lots of other cool stuff, too!
